PayBOXX (alpha/v1.0.2) guide

PayBOXX is a web interface for an intuitive interaction with blockchains, intended to make payment process easy, quick and intuitive. This application allows users to generate addresses, choose a cryptocurrency, and keep full control of funds without involving a third party.

How to send coins using PayBOXX?

  • Pick the currency you want to send.
  • Import the private key associated with your wallet address.
  • Fill in the correct destination address or use the QRcode scanner by clicking on the camera icon. ( In iOS make sure to grand permission in Safari settings for the camera to work.)
  • Enter the amount you want to transact.
  • After this your transaction fee will be calculated.
  • Send coins by simply clicking the Send button.
  • Confirm the transaction.

How to generate a new wallet address?

  • Click the New button next to the private key field
  • After popup window has appeared, select Generate.
  • Copy and paste in a safe place or write down the following information: address, public key, private key.
  • Remember: NEVER share your private address.
